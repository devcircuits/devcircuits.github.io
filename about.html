About DevCircuit

At DevCircuit, we are architects of innovation, dedicated to shaping the future of connected technologies. Founded with a passion for engineering excellence, our mission is to empower businesses and communities with cutting-edge IoT, edge computing, and embedded systems solutions.

Headquartered in India, we combine global expertise with local insight, delivering transformative hardware and software that redefine industries.

Our Expertise

Our team of seasoned engineers, designers, and innovators specializes in creating custom circuits, firmware, and applications that drive efficiency, sustainability, and scalability. From smart cities to precision agriculture, we partner with clients to turn ambitious ideas into reality, leveraging platforms like ESP32, ARM, and Shakti V (RISC-V).

Our Commitment

With a commitment to open-source innovation and rigorous quality standards, we ensure every solution is future-proof and impactful. At the heart of DevCircuit lies a vision to democratize technology, making advanced systems accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Our Vision

Whether you're a startup pioneering a new IoT device or an enterprise optimizing industrial processes, we provide end-to-end support, from concept to production. Join us in building a smarter, more connected world—let's circuit the future together.
