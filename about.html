About Us

Welcome to Design, Engineering, Vision Circuit—DEV Circuit—where innovation meets precision in the world of cutting-edge technology. We are a dynamic engineering and design organization specializing in partnering with product companies to deliver end-to-end solutions in circuit design, printed circuit board (PCB) development, firmware coding, and associated application development. With a relentless focus on quality, creativity, and technological advancement, we empower businesses to bring their ideas to life—efficiently, reliably, and at scale.

Our Expertise

At DEV Circuit, we pride ourselves on being industry leaders in designing and developing Internet of Things (IoT) and Edge devices. Our team excels in harnessing the power of modern connectivity standards, including Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT) and CAT-M LTE, to create smart, energy-efficient, and scalable solutions tailored to the needs of today’s interconnected world. Whether it’s enabling real-time data processing at the edge or building robust IoT ecosystems, we deliver products that redefine performance and reliability.

Our deep technical proficiency spans a wide range of platforms, with specialized expertise in ESP32 and ARM-based architectures—two of the most versatile and widely adopted systems in embedded design. From low-power IoT sensors to high-performance edge computing devices, we leverage these platforms to craft solutions that are both innovative and cost-effective. Additionally, our pioneering work with the Shakti V processor—a RISC-V-based architecture developed in India—demonstrates our commitment to pushing the boundaries of open-source hardware and fostering self-reliance in the global tech landscape.

What We Do

We collaborate closely with product companies, acting as an extension of their teams to transform concepts into market-ready solutions. Our comprehensive services include:

  • Circuit Design & PCB Development: Crafting efficient, high-quality circuits and PCBs optimized for performance, manufacturability, and scalability.
  • Firmware Development: Writing robust, secure, and optimized firmware code to breathe life into hardware, ensuring seamless functionality and integration.
  • Application Development: Building intuitive, user-centric applications that complement hardware solutions and enhance end-user experiences.
  • IoT & Edge Solutions: Designing connected devices that thrive in challenging environments, from smart cities to industrial automation.
  • Research & Innovation: Conducting deep research to stay ahead of industry trends and deliver future-proof technologies.
